About

About Spring Lodge

Spring Lodge is located in the prime area of Nyaung Shwe Town which is 0.8 km away from Mingalar market and located just diagonally of the VIP Jetty. With 7 duplex bungalows and each of the bungalow has 2 rooms, and a small swimming pool ( 5 m x 13 m) Spring Lodge is a treasure for those who are looking for a quiet place at a prime area of the town.


Accessibility

By flights

One may reach to Spring Lodge by flights to Heho airport then just continue by taxi to Nyaung Shwe for about 40 minutes ( 30 km).
